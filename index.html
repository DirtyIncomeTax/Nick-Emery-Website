Introduction

I am a 2nd year undergraduate student at the Georgia Institute of Technology seeking a degree in Computational Media, with a concentration in People and Games. I hope to,one day, put my military experience and technical education to work in the world of video games and digital media.

Simply put: I adore video games. My entire life, I have loved media; movies, music, comics, art, but none do I love more than games. Since I can remember, I have wanted to work in the games indusrty. I don't just want to help make games, I want to be involved at every level of development - art, design, production, narrative, everything. I want to re-create those feelings I had when I first stepped onto the ring-world of Halo or explored ancient ruins in Uncharted for a new generation of players. Even through my years in the Marines, I always held onto the dream of, one day, working at a studio creating a new world for gamers to explore. At Georgie Tech, I hope to gain all the skills, resources, and knowledge I need to make that dream a reality.
